## Collpsible frame for Tkinter or Collapsible panel 
Originally published: 2017-03-04 00:34:09 
Last updated: 2017-03-06 12:40:28 
Author: Miguel Martínez López 
 
Click on button and frame will collapse or expand.\n\nIf you want to fit the frame to width of contents, call method "udpate_width()".\n\nSimple API:\n\n- open()\n- close()\n- toggle()\n- update_width()
